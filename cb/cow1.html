Cow's & Bulls's

How to play ?

This game is mind based game which improve's your IQ .Now lets know how to play.

COW=== C

BULL === B

point - 1

The main thing is computer select's a random number you have to crack that number. Clues will be given to you. Other important thing is that every digit in number is to be distinct i.e number's like 121 is not allowed.

point - 2

Very easy level means computer select's a 2 digit number i.e number between 10 & 99 , you should crack it.

point - 3

Easy level means computer select's a 3 digit number i.e number between 100 & 999 , you should crack it.

point - 4

Medium level means computer select's a 4 digit number i.e number between 1000 & 9999 , you should crack it.

point - 5

Hard level means computer select's a 5 digit number i.e number between 10000 & 99999 , you should crack it.

point - 6

What means cow's and bull's ?

for example you are playing medium level and if you guessed number as 1234 and computer's selection is 1824
here we can notice that 1 and 4 is present in same place in your number and computer selection So,

NUMBER OF BULL'S = 2

here 2 is common in your number and computer selection but it is not in the same position So,

NUMBER OF COW'S = 1

point - 7

By seeing cow's and bull's you should change your guess number by getting 4 BULLS i.e guess number == computer selection you win.
