About Us

Club mathematica is one of the most prominent and actively functioning clubs of National Institute Of Technology Calicut. With its foundation laid in the early 2000's, the club keep flourishing year by year because of the strong passion and determination of its members.

Club Mathematica has been helping young minds improve their logical reasoning skills and to think out of the box, guiding them to explore math like never before.

Providing a platform for students to grow beyond academics in all of its ventures, Club Mathematica has constantly proved its excellent work each time.

Events

SPERANZA

Speranza was a fun-filled and exhilarating event conducted as an entertaining online inter-branch competition exclusively for the first year students of NIT Calicut where the contestants from each branch battle against each other to prove their mettle.During the challenging period of a frightful pandemic, the club stepped up, proving to make the best out of every opportunity, by hosting Speranza in an exciting virtual concept as its first ever fully online event. Moreover, Speranza served a greater good by raising funds for Kerala Government's CoVID relief fund.

LOCUS

Launching its first edition in 2019, Locus emerged as a technical summit that explores the richness in mathematics. Comprising of interactive sessions, workshops and intriguing lectures Locus helped give students an insight on Cryptography, Machine Learning, Vedic Mathematics, Finance Modelling and various other concepts, providing a platform for technically driven students to expand their horizons and sharpen their skills.

INFINITUM

Infinitum is an annual inter-school quiz competition that evaluates the logical and reasoning skills of students , providing them a memorable experience infused with fun and healthy competition Taking its roots in 2011 as a small endeavour in Calicut, Infinitum has now expanded its realm to all zones of Kerala, Andhra Pradesh, Telangana and even to UAE! Following the preliminary rounds, the best of the lot is screened out for the Winner title by competing against each other in the district and state level competitions.
