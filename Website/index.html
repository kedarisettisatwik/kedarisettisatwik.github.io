Club Mathematicia


Club mathematica is one of the most prominent and actively
functioning clubs of National Institute Of Technology Calicut. With
its foundation laid in the early 2000's,the club keep flourishing year
by year because of the strong passion and determination of its
members.

Club Mathematica has been helping young minds improve their
logical reasoning skills and to think out of the box,guiding them to
explore math like never before.

Providing a platform for students to grow beyond academics in all of
its ventures, Club Mathematica has constantly proved its excellent
work each time.
